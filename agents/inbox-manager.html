What the Inbox Manager does

The Inbox Manager connects to your inbox read-only, reads every incoming message, and classifies it by intent — hot lead, booking, pricing question, decline, junk. It drafts a one-click reply in your voice for everything worth answering and pushes the messages that need you today to the top of the queue. You stop digging through email; you review and hit send. It never sends anything for you.
