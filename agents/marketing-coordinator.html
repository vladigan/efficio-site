What this AI team owns

Each responsibility below maps to something a human in this role would do. We deploy the AI to handle it the same way you'd want a person to — except it doesn't sleep, quit, or take 6 weeks to ramp.

Content drafting
Blog posts, service-page copy, and post captions written in your voice from what you actually do — not generic filler. You approve; it queues.
Social posting
A consistent cadence across the channels you use. Before-and-afters, tips, team moments — drafted, scheduled, and posted so your feed never goes quiet for three months.
Review generation
Asks happy customers for a review at the right moment — a personalized message after a great job — when they're most likely to say yes. The single biggest lever on local ranking, worked every day.
Review response
Drafts replies to new reviews in your tone — gracious to the good ones, calm and fixing-it to the bad ones. Flags the borderline ones to you privately before they go public.
Email campaigns
Newsletters, seasonal offers, and re-engagement emails to your existing list — written, segmented, and sent. The list you've been meaning to email for a year, finally working.
Local SEO
Google Business Profile kept current, posts published, categories and hours accurate, questions answered. The unglamorous upkeep that decides whether you show up in the map pack.
