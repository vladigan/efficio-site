What this AI hire owns

Each responsibility below maps to something a human in this role would do. We deploy the AI to handle it the same way you'd want a person to — except it doesn't sleep, quit, or take 6 weeks to ramp.

New-client intake
The moment a deal closes, the intake goes out — the right form, the right questions, in your voice. No "we'll send paperwork next week," no momentum lost.
Doc & data collection
Chases the documents and details you need to start — politely, on a cadence, until they're all in. The follow-ups nobody on your team has time to send.
Kickoff scheduling
Books the kickoff against your real availability, sends the prep, and reminds both sides so the call actually happens. No back-and-forth, no no-shows.
Account setup
Creates the records, folders, and access the new client needs — in your CRM and tools — so nothing is improvised on day one.
Welcome sequence
A warm, well-paced welcome that sets expectations, answers the obvious questions, and tells them exactly what happens next — so they feel taken care of, not abandoned.
First-value delivery
Tracks each new client to their first real win and flags anyone who stalls. The difference between a client who sticks and one who quietly ghosts.
