What this AI team owns

Each responsibility below maps to something a human in this role would do. We deploy the AI to handle it the same way you'd want a person to — except it doesn't sleep, quit, or take 6 weeks to ramp.

Post-service follow-up
After every job, visit, or delivery, a follow-up goes out — checking in, surfacing the next thing they'll need, keeping you top of mind instead of forgotten.
Review requests
Asks for the review at the moment they're happiest — right after a great experience — in your voice. The single biggest lever on reputation, worked every time, not when someone remembers.
Lapsed re-engagement
Watches for customers overdue for their next visit or order against your normal cadence, and reaches out before they drift to a competitor. The dormant list, worked weekly.
Win-back
For customers who've gone quiet, a genuine win-back — a reason to come back, in the right tone, at the right time. The revenue that's already yours, recovered.
NPS & feedback
Sends the check-in, reads the answer, and routes it — happy customers to a review, unhappy ones privately to you before they post a one-star.
Referral asks
Asks your best customers for a referral when they've just told you they're delighted — the warmest lead source you have, finally asked for systematically.

What it looks like in your business

Same AI team, different business, different sample interaction. We configure the Customer Retention Team to your cadence, your tools, your customers, and your voice — on the audit call.

Home & field services

Six months after a tune-up, a friendly reminder that maintenance is due. A review asked for the day after a five-star job. The customers you served once become the ones who call you first — and refer their neighbor.

Dental or healthcare practice

Recall list of lapsed patients worked weekly — overdue cleanings and check-ups nudged back onto the schedule. Feedback collected after visits, with unhappy ones routed to the office before they hit Google.

Beauty & wellness

Clients overdue for their usual cadence get a warm rebook nudge with a one-click link. Win-backs to the regulars who drifted. Review asks timed to a great appointment — quiet revenue that compounds.

Sales-driven business

Renewals flagged ahead of time and worked, not missed. Past buyers re-engaged before they shop around. Referrals asked for at the peak of a happy relationship instead of never.
