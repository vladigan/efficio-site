The 3 components of your AI-Readiness score

Total = Pain Score (0–40) + Volume Signal (0–30) + Savings Ceiling (0–30). Higher means more owner/staff hours currently going to work an AI agent could handle in the background.

40/100

Pain Score

Auto-generated from absence of modern ops tools on your public footprint.

  • No chatbot / lead capture on website
  • No online booking system
  • No CRM signals (form submission, email capture)
  • No follow-up automation visible
  • No social-proof loops (review automation)
30/100

Volume Signal

Your shop's footprint × demand intensity. The bigger your operation, the more an automation gap hurts.

  • Google review count weighted
  • Star rating bonus (4.0+)
  • Implied throughput per vertical
  • Web traffic + SEO indicators
30/100

Savings Ceiling

Estimated $/mo in owner/staff time currently going to manual admin. Scaled to your business size.

  • Hours/week on intake & follow-up
  • Hourly cost per saved hour
  • Revenue leak from slow response
  • Recurring cadence opportunities
