AI for accounting and bookkeeping firms: end the document chase

Brady Gay · May 20, 2026 · ~3 min read

Ask any accountant where the time goes and you'll hear the same answer: chasing clients for documents, fielding the same questions over and over, and onboarding new clients one repetitive email at a time. None of it is the actual accounting — it's the friction around it, and it only gets heavier as you grow.

End the document chase

An AI Back Office team owns the document chase: it requests what's missing, follows up until it arrives, and files it where it belongs — so you're not the one sending the fourth "just following up" email.

Onboard new clients without the back-and-forth

An AI Client Onboarding team handles new-client intake end to end: the engagement docs, the data collection, the kickoff — so a new client is productive in days, not weeks of back-and-forth. And an AI Inbox / Back Office team triages the recurring questions, surfacing only what actually needs your expertise.

The result: you spend your hours on billable work and advisory, not administrative chase. The teams learn your firm — your client types, your document checklists, your deadlines. We sell AI teams, not AI tools — you're not buying another app to manage, you're handing the chase to a team that owns it.
