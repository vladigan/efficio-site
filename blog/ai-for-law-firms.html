AI for law firms: stop losing new-matter calls and drowning in admin

Brady Gay · May 20, 2026 · ~3 min read

For a small law firm, two things quietly cost you the most: the new-matter calls that go to voicemail while you're in court or with a client, and the administrative weight that eats the hours you could bill.

Capture every new-matter call

A potential client with a legal problem doesn't wait — if no one answers, they call the next firm on the list. An AI Front Office team answers every call, captures the matter details, runs the initial conflict-check questions, and books the consult — so the lead your reputation earned actually becomes a client.

Take the admin off your plate

Behind that, an AI Back Office team owns the admin: intake paperwork, scheduling, document filing, deadline tracking, the daily what-needs-you brief. And an AI Finance team handles the part most firms hate — invoicing and chasing AR — so the work you've done actually gets paid on time.

None of this replaces your judgment or your relationships; it removes the friction around them. The teams are trained on your firm — your practice areas, your intake rules, your tone — so clients get fast, professional responses and you get your time back for the work that needs a lawyer. We sell AI teams, not AI tools — you hand over the function, not another login to run.
