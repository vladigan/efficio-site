01 · Who we are

Not a tool. Not a vendor. Your AI team.

Most AI offers hand you a login. We hand you a team. Efficio is a managed AI service that embeds inside small and medium businesses and operates the way an in-house engineer would, if you could afford to hire one. You tell us what's eating the day. We build the AI that handles it. We deploy it inside your business and we run it. You don't lift a finger.

Efficio is a US-based AI engineering team that builds, deploys, and operates AI inside service businesses. Our own back office runs on the exact same stack we'd build for you — the outreach you may have received, the reply triage, the dashboards. Nothing in our pitch is theoretical; you're seeing the product on day one.

"Small businesses can't afford a CTO and don't have time to learn AI tools. You tell us what's killing your time. We build and run the AI that fixes it. You don't touch a line of code. You don't manage a vendor. You just stop doing the thing."

We work cross-vertical: home services, trades, professional services, healthcare, real estate, retail, accounting, agencies. Any business where owner time is the bottleneck and a narrow AI module removes hours/week. We are deliberately not industry-specific. The playbook is the same shape regardless — the customization happens inside it.

03 · The 3 AI departments

Three departments, seven teams. We bring an AI team for each function.

An AI team for each function of your business. Same seven teams every customer can draw on — what changes is which ones you start with and which custom builds layer on top.

01

AI Receptionist

Front office

Answers every call, text, and web form in seconds — 24 hours a day. Books appointments, captures the details, flags emergencies, never sends a lead to voicemail. The single highest-return agent for any service business that gets inbound calls.

What it does

  • Answers inbound calls in under 10 seconds
  • Texts back web-form leads in <1 minute
  • Books appointments directly into your calendar
  • Captures address, equipment, urgency, callback number
  • Routes emergencies to a live human immediately
  • Logs every interaction so nothing falls through

Sample interaction

Caller: "My water heater stopped working — can someone come out?"  →  AI: "I can get a plumber to you. Free Thursday morning or afternoon?"  →  Booked Thursday 9-11am. Text confirmation sent.

Who needs this most

  • HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping
  • Dental and medical practices
  • Real estate teams
  • Any service business where a missed call is a missed job
02

AI Sales Navigator

Sales

Responds to new leads instantly, builds itemized quotes from a photo + a few specs, follows up until they decide. This is where money quietly dies in most service businesses — quotes go out and nothing happens. We close that loop.

What it does

  • First-touch response in under 5 minutes
  • Builds itemized quotes against your pricing book
  • Sends, opens, tracks — you see when they read it
  • Automatic follow-up: Day 2 check-in, Day 5 nudge, Day 10 final
  • Re-engages cold leads from months ago
  • Flags high-value leads for personal follow-up

Sample interaction

Customer texts photo of old AC unit + sq ft  →  AI drafts 3-tier itemized install quote against your price book  →  you approve tier 2  →  sent in 90 seconds. Customer opens it twice.

Who needs this most

  • Any business that sends estimates / proposals / quotes
  • HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, construction
  • Law firms, accounting firms (intake + scope)
  • Agencies and consultants
03

AI Bookkeeper

Accounting

AR chase, expense categorization, daily reconciliation, monthly close prep, invoice generation. Reads QuickBooks / Stripe / your bank. Books close by the 3rd of the month, not the 15th.

What it does

  • Drafts the right-tone AR follow-up for each overdue invoice (polite at 7d, firm at 21d, escalation at 45d)
  • Daily reconciliation between Stripe / bank / QuickBooks — flags drift instantly
  • Auto-categorizes every transaction into the right GL bucket
  • Drafts invoices from completed jobs / matters / engagements
  • Month-end close prep: 92%-ready report by the 3rd
  • Clean handoff to your CPA at tax time

Sample interaction

QuickBooks shows 14 invoices > 7 days overdue  →  AI drafts polite follow-ups in your voice, references each invoice's specific service  →  9 paid within 48 hours of send. The owner never had to chase.

Who needs this most

  • Service businesses with $50K+ MRR running QuickBooks / Xero
  • Anyone whose AR aging report makes them anxious
  • Owners doing books at 11pm on Sunday
  • Practices / firms with insurance or third-party AR cycles
04

AI Office Manager

Back office + Operations

Everything happening inside your business. Triages the inbox, files documents, owns scheduling logistics, routes internal handoffs, watches the operation for stalled work, builds the daily-brief that runs your morning. The role you'd hire a $45–90K/yr human office manager for — covering admin + ops in one seat.

What it does

  • Classifies every inbound message by intent and drafts replies in your voice
  • Files documents, routes vendor / billing emails, runs internal handoffs
  • Watches for stalled work (customer file idle past your SLA) and pings the right human
  • Tracks the KPIs that matter (jobs/wk, win rate, AR aging, NPS)
  • Compiles a 6-line daily brief by 7am
  • Reviews + retention work: asks happy customers for reviews, surfaces unhappy ones privately

Sample interaction

7am Monday: Office Manager drops a 6-line brief in your inbox — "4 jobs shipped over the weekend, 2 quotes need your approval, 1 stalled handoff caught Friday at 4:48pm and re-routed, AR clean."

Who needs this most

  • Owners drowning in their own inbox
  • Operations past $200K MRR with multiple staff
  • Anyone whose inbox runs their day instead of the other way around
  • Teams where stalled handoffs cost customers
