The work that keeps you out of the CEO seat

Every one of these is work a coordinated AI team should be handling — not the founder.

A prospect fills out your form while you're heads-down editing or on a client call. By the time you surface, they've booked with someone faster.

Follow-up is slow and lumpy

Warm leads go quiet because nobody nudged them on day two and day five. Revenue swings month to month because follow-up depends on you finding the time.

You're stuck in fulfillment instead of growth

What the AI team handles

One coordinated team — not a pile of tools

A single bot you have to operate is an AI tool. That's what everyone else sells. We build and run a GROUP of agents — a team — that owns a whole function and runs it daily. Here's what that looks like for an agency.

Inbound lead routing — Front Office team

New inquiries get answered, qualified, and routed in seconds — while you're on a client call. Nothing waits for you to surface.

Sales follow-up cadence — Growth team

Warm leads get a structured, on-brand follow-up sequence so they don't go quiet. Your pipeline stops depending on whether you remembered to chase.

Client onboarding — Back Office team

New accounts get intake forms, welcome sequences, asset requests, and kickoff scheduling handled automatically — the same clean start every time.

Project status updates

Clients get proactive progress updates so they stop emailing "any update?" — and you stop being the human status board.

Invoicing reminders

Payment nudges go out on schedule so receivables don't slip and you're not the one chasing money.

Missed-call text-back

Step into a meeting and miss a call? The Front Office team texts back instantly, captures the need, and books the next step.
