The problem

You're not losing to competitors. You're losing to your own missed calls.

Every small business leaks the same way. A call goes to voicemail during a job. A web lead sits until tomorrow and books with whoever answered first. A quote goes out and nobody follows up a second time. None of it is dramatic — it's just quiet, daily loss that never shows up on a report.

Hiring more admin help doesn't fix it. People go home at 5, take weekends, and cost more every year. And the "AI tools" pitched at this problem mostly hand you software and a login, then leave you to figure it out — which is exactly why most of them end up unused.

The fix isn't another tool. It's someone who builds the automation into your business, runs it for you, and stands behind it.

Slow follow-up

Owner as bottleneck

Scheduling, reminders, chasing reviews, re-typing the same answers. It's all on you, and it doesn't scale.

Questions

Fair questions, straight answers

How does "pay only if it works" actually work?

On the first call we agree, in writing, on what the build has to do — the specific jobs your AI team will handle. You pay nothing upfront. Once it's live and doing that job, billing starts. If it isn't doing what we agreed, you don't pay and we either fix it or part ways. No contracts holding you hostage.

Is this just a chatbot?

No. Chatbots wait on your website for someone to type at them. Your AI team is wired into your phone line, calendar, CRM, and inbox — it answers calls, sends texts, books real appointments, and follows up on its own. And it's built and supervised by human engineers, not spun up from a template.

What if it doesn't work for my business?

Then you don't pay — that's the whole arrangement. In practice, we protect against this by scoping honestly: if we don't think we can automate something well for your business, we'll say so on the first call instead of taking the deal.

Will customers know they're talking to AI?

If they ask, it's honest — we don't build anything that pretends to be a person when asked directly. But it's trained on your business, your services, and your tone, and its job is to be genuinely useful: answer the question, take the details, book the time. Anything complex or sensitive gets handed to you or your staff.

Do I need to switch software or learn anything new?

No. We build inside the tools you already run — your phone system, your calendar, your CRM. If something's missing, we add the smallest possible piece, and we manage it. Your day-to-day doesn't change, except the busywork stops landing on you.

What does it cost after it's live?

A flat monthly rate, set during scoping based on what we build — you'll know the exact number before we start, and it's a fraction of a part-time hire. No usage surprises, no per-minute metering.
