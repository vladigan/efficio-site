How It Works

From first call to live system — usually in 3 weeks.

A transparent, four-stage process. Fixed scope. Fixed price. No surprises, no "phase 2 to be quoted later." You see progress every week, and you see something working by the end of week one.

1
Stage 01 20 minutes · Free

Discovery call

A no-pitch, no-obligation 20-minute call. We ask three questions: what's slow or painful in your business right now, what have you already tried, and what's in your current stack. We listen, we ask follow-ups, and we form a quick working hypothesis of where AI fits and where it doesn't.

If we can't help — wrong size, wrong problem, wrong stage — we'll tell you on the call and point you somewhere useful. Most calls end one of three ways: proposal incoming, a specific recommendation we can't help with, or a "come back in 6 months when X is true." All three are valid.

You leave the call with
  • A clear read on whether there's a fit
  • 1–2 concrete opportunities we've flagged
  • An estimate range for the work if we proceed
  • Zero obligation and no follow-up spam
2
Stage 02 2–4 business days

Written scope & proposal

If there's a fit, we come back within a few days with a written scope. It spells out exactly what we'll build, exactly what you're paying, and exactly what "done" looks like. Payment is split — 50% to kick off, 50% on delivery. Month-to-month retainer terms if that's the arrangement.

Fixed price means no change-order ambushes mid-build. If the scope legitimately grows after we're underway (you want an extra view, a new integration), we quote that as an add-on so you decide — we don't decide for you.

You get
  • Written project scope with acceptance criteria
  • Itemized deliverables and timeline
  • Fixed price for the build
  • Clear retainer terms (if applicable)
3
Stage 03 2–4 weeks

Build & first working cut

Kickoff, access provisioned, work begins. By the end of week 1, there is a working version in your hands — rough, incomplete, but real. You click through it, tell us what's wrong, what's missing, what's surprising. We iterate from there.

You see progress weekly. Async updates on Slack, email, or whichever channel you prefer. One 30-minute sync per week only if you want one — most clients don't.

By the end
  • Live system running under your domain / infrastructure
  • Full source code in a repo you control
  • Runbook + documentation
  • Monitoring and error-reporting wired up
  • Handoff walkthrough with your team
4
Stage 04 Month-to-month, optional

Retainer & iteration

Most clients want us to keep building after launch — the initial build surfaces the next five things they want. That's when we roll into a monthly retainer. Month-to-month, cancel anytime, a set number of hours per month, priorities set by you. No 12-month commitments, no punitive exit clauses.

Don't want ongoing support? Fine. Everything's documented, the code is yours, and you can take it in-house or hand it to another vendor. We designed delivery so you could fire us cleanly — which, incidentally, is why very few clients do.

Retainer tiers
  • Growth — $2,500/mo · ~20 hrs, one agent or dashboard ongoing
  • Full Stack — $3,500/mo · ~30 hrs, agents + dashboards evolving in parallel
  • Dedicated — $7,500/mo · ~60 hrs, we act as your AI team

Common questions about how we work

Who owns the code we build together?

You do. Full source, your repository, your infrastructure, your data. If Efficio shut down tomorrow, nothing you have would break and nothing would disappear. We design delivery explicitly so you can fire us without penalty.

What if the scope changes mid-project?

Small in-scope changes — wording, styling, tweaks — are included. Structural changes get quoted as add-ons, transparently, before we build them. You decide whether to proceed. No surprise bills.

How does payment work?

Builds: 50% to kick off, 50% on delivery. Retainers: monthly, first month prorated, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Stripe checkout for convenience; wire or ACH if you prefer.

Do you work nights/weekends for rush projects?

Not usually. Rushed AI builds produce fragile AI builds. If you truly need same-week turnaround, we'll tell you honestly what we can deliver in that window — which is often less than you need.

Where's the team based?

Miami-based. Work is done async across US time zones. We've done video calls from 7am to 9pm ET, and we'll meet where you're comfortable.

Can you work within our security / compliance requirements?

Usually yes — dedicated VPCs, SSO, SOC 2 paperwork, data residency. The exception is heavily regulated spaces (healthcare PHI, financial SOC 1, government clearance) where we'll want to talk about your specific stack before committing.

Let's figure out if we're a fit.

20 minutes, free, no pitch. You tell us what's slow or painful. We tell you whether we can help — and if we can't, we tell you that too.
