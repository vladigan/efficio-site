§ 04 — Straight answers

Things creators actually ask.

01Will my data stay private? I’m on OnlyFans.+

Yes. NDA on day one. Read-only access — we never log in as you, never post, never touch DMs. Your data lives in a private workspace only your assigned team sees. Discretion is the default. We work with adult creators every week and treat OF revenue exactly like Patreon revenue: a number on a P&L.

02Can you actually aggregate every platform?+

Yes. AdSense, Patreon, OnlyFans, Stripe, PayPal, Twitch payouts, TikTok Creator Fund, brand-deal invoices, merch storefronts, affiliate dashboards. If a platform has no API, we ingest CSVs weekly. By month two you can see — in one chart — which platform actually paid.

03Do you replace my CPA?+

No. We make your CPA’s life easier. We hand them a clean P&L, categorized expenses, and a running tax provision so April isn’t a surprise. If you don’t have a CPA, we’ll refer one who works with creators. If you have a great one, we make their bill smaller.

04What if I want to switch back?+

Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. We hand back every report, dashboard export, and reconciled record. No lock-in, no breakup fee. We’d rather earn the next month than trap you.

05Do you take a cut of my brand deals?+

Never. One flat monthly fee. We tell you what a brand deal is worth based on your CPM, audience, and recent deals in your niche — you sign, you keep 100%. No conflict of interest, no incentive to push you into bad partnerships.
