5 AI agents we'd ship for GCs

What an AI team looks like for a 6-PM commercial GC.

Each agent runs a workflow your superintendent used to handle from a truck cab. They talk to Procore / Buildertrend / Autodesk Build, your accounting system, your sub portals.

Daily-report compiler

Pulls field photos + crew logs + safety notes from the super's phone, drafts the daily, routes to the PM for one-click approval. Owner sees it before COB.

Sub coordinator

Watches the project schedule. When a predecessor finishes, the next sub gets a structured handoff with site photos + access info + scope summary. No more 4-text negotiation.

RFI tracker

Every RFI is timestamped + routed to the right design-team member with reminders. Aging RFIs get escalated automatically. Project schedule stops slipping over a question that 'someone asked last Tuesday'.

Change-order router

Field requests get costed (subcontractor markups + GC fee), routed for owner approval, and logged to the project record — without a PM rebuilding the spreadsheet.

Owner-update agent

Weekly project digest with photos, schedule status, RFI/change-order activity, and budget. Owners stop calling for updates. PMs stop drafting them.
