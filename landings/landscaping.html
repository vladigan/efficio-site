5 AI agents we'd ship for landscaping

What an AI team looks like for an 8-crew operation.

Each agent runs a workflow your office used to handle by phone tag during the spring rush. They talk to your scheduling tool, your billing system, your CRM.

Spring-intake qualifier

Qualifies inbound leads by scope + zip + budget hint, slots them into estimator routes. Tire-kickers get a polite drip; real prospects get same-day callback.

Route optimizer

Daily crew routing built around drive time, job complexity, and crew skill. Saves 15–20% of crew windshield time without a fleet manager.

Recurring-service tracker

Watches every weekly / bi-weekly / monthly contract. Flags missed visits, expiring contracts, and add-on opportunities. Your maintenance revenue stops slipping.

Weather-reschedule agent

When rain hits, every contract gets the right rescheduled-day text. Customers don't call you wondering. Crews don't show up to wet sites.

Estimate-followup agent

Every quote gets a structured 24h / 5-day / 14-day nudge. Pricing questions route to the owner. Quotes don't die in a customer's inbox.
