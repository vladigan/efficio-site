The thesis in one line

Efficio is the AI team that meets you where you are and grows with you. Crawl → walk → run → sprint. We're the only managed AI service that serves a $50K/mo shop and a $5M/mo operation off the same backend — because the AI is ours, not yours, and we run and grow it for you.

What we sell

A contracted AI team you hire. Not software. Not a tool. Not "training." Real AI teams we build, deploy, and run inside your business — each mapped to a function on your org chart.

Who we're for

$30K/mo to $5M/mo SMBs. Owner-operated. Ops-heavy. Tired of being told "you're too small for us."

Verticals where we win: Home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping) · Healthcare practices (dental, medical, chiro, vet, optometry) · Professional services (law, accounting, insurance, consulting) · Real estate teams · Restaurants & hospitality · Beauty & wellness · Cleaning · Auto & transport · General trades · Creative & event services.

Not for: Enterprise (refer to QueryNow) · adult / creator economy · anyone who refuses read-only OAuth access · tire-kickers shopping for "AI tools to play with."

The differentiator (one paragraph)

Most AI agencies hand you software you have to learn, tools you have to figure out, or "training" that becomes your problem to implement. Efficio is different. You OAuth your existing tools (Stripe, QuickBooks, your CRM, Google Workspace), drop documents in a private Dropbox folder, and we take it from there. Total customer effort across the entire onboarding: ~25 minutes. Zero ongoing data entry. First AI team live in 7 days. If we miss day 7, the month is on us.

Contractual guarantees (these print on every page)

  • Day-7 promise. First AI team live in your business within 7 days of kickoff. Miss it → month is free.
  • Day-30 refund. Metric we agreed on must move by day 30. If not → full first-month refund. No debate.
  • Month-to-month. Cancel any month. No contract. No penalty.
  • Read-only access. We never see passwords. You revoke any time from inside each tool.
  • Your data, your cockpit. Per-customer runtime. Never blended, never sold, never used to train anything.

The funnel

  1. efficio.tech/qualify — 8 questions, 60 seconds, routes to a tier + books the audit call
  2. 30-min audit call. We name the workflow we'd ship in week one + the metric we'll move by day 30.
  3. OAuth onboarding (~10 min) + Dropbox doc drop (~5 min)
  4. Day 5 — Audit Report + cockpit live with their real data flowing in
  5. Day 7+ — first AI team running, weekly digest hits their inbox every Monday 8am ET

Voice rules

Always

  • "We're your AI team." The tagline.
  • "We do it. You operate."
  • Direct · premium · conversational. Short sentences.
  • Real numbers cited to real sources.

Banned words

  • "synergy" · "leverage" · "10x" · "unlock" · "transform" · "AI-powered"
  • "tool" · "platform" · "software" · "SaaS" — those are anti-frames. We're a service. We sell people, not products.
  • Fabricated testimonials. Fake "Trusted by" logos. Made-up customer counts.
  • Centered SaaS hero pattern (H1 + sub + two buttons). We use editorial.
  • Adult / OnlyFans / creator-economy references publicly.
