Pricing questions.

01Is the setup fee really one-time?+

Yes. One-time. Covers integrations, dashboard build, historical data import, NDA, contract setup, and the first AI agent. After that you’re on the monthly fee — no hidden lines.

02Can I switch tiers later?+

Yes. Up or down, anytime. The setup-fee difference at a higher tier is pro-rated against what you already paid. Most owners start on Specialist and move to AI Team between months 3 and 6 as the first agent proves itself.

03Do you charge per seat? Per agent? Per call?+

Never. One flat monthly fee. Doesn’t matter if the agent handles 200 calls or 2,000 calls a week, or how many of your staff log into the dashboard. The flat-fee model is the whole point.

04Annual discount?+

Yes — 15% off for annual prepay, on any tier. Talk to Brady on the call if you want it.
