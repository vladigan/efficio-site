// before you commit

Pricing questions, answered.

Is there a contract or setup fee?

No. Every tier is month-to-month with $0 upfront and no build fee. Cancel anytime — you're never locked in.

What if it doesn't work for my business?

You only start paying once your first build is live and doing the work, and you get measurable proof in the first 30 days or your money back. The risk sits with us, not you.

Can I start small and move up later?

Yes — start where you're hurting most. Many owners begin on Starter ($497/mo) or a single team, prove it out, then scale up a tier when they're ready. No penalty for changing.

What am I actually paying for each month?

We build and run the AI workforce for you — not another tool you have to operate. Every tier includes your live dashboard; the number of AI teams, custom builds, and strategy-call cadence step up as you go higher.

How fast until something is live?

Your dashboard goes live around day 5 and your first agent or tool around day 7 — not a months-long implementation.
