Pricing questions.

01Is the setup fee really one-time?+

Yes. One-time. Covers integrations, dashboard build, historical data import, NDA, contract setup, account-team kickoff. After that you’re on the monthly fee — no hidden lines.

02Can I switch tiers later?+

Yes. Up or down, anytime. The setup fee at a higher tier is pro-rated against what you already paid. Most creators move from Solo to Team between months 4 and 8 as their operation grows.

03Do you take any percentage of my revenue?+

Never. One flat monthly fee. We’re not a manager. We don’t take a cut of brand deals, ad revenue, subscriptions, sponsorships — nothing. The flat-fee model is the whole point.

04Annual discount?+

Yes — 15% off for annual prepay, on any tier. Talk to Brady on the audit call if you want it.
