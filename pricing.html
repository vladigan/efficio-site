Which service line is right for me?

Pick Dashboard Builds if your team spends hours reconciling spreadsheets, your data is siloed across three or more tools, or your Monday status meeting is a copy-paste exercise. Fixed-scope, one-time price, 2–6 week delivery.

Pick AI Automation if owner/operator time is your bottleneck — manual quoting, slow intake, repetitive email, missed follow-ups, or any workflow where "if we just had a bot for this…" keeps coming up. Month-to-month retainer, cancel anytime.

Many clients do both. A dashboard makes the AI automation visible in real time, and the AI automation fills the dashboard with actual conversions to measure.
