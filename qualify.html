60-second qualifier

Find your tier in 60 seconds.

8 questions about your revenue, your tools, and where time is leaking. We route you to the right starting tier — Starter, AI Specialist, AI Team, or AI Department — and book your free audit call.

Question 1 of 8
What's your monthly revenue?
Rough estimate. Doesn't have to be exact — we'll pull the real number from your books on the audit call.
Question 2 of 8
How do you take payments?
If it's Stripe / Square / PayPal we can plug in fast. Cash-only means we set up your stack first.
Question 3 of 8
What's your CRM / customer software?
The thing that holds your customer list and their history. Spreadsheet counts — we'll fix that.
Question 4 of 8
How does the bookkeeping happen?
Where the money math lives. Manual is fine — we just need to know.
Question 5 of 8
What's eating the most time right now?
Pick the one workflow that, if fixed, would buy back the most hours.
Question 6 of 8
How many hours/week on admin work?
Hours the owner (or someone you're paying) spends on stuff that isn't growing the business.
Question 7 of 8
Running paid ads currently?
Helps us know if we're plugging into existing traffic or rebuilding the funnel.
