Refund Policy & Guarantee

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Two promises sit behind every Efficio subscription: your first AI team is live in seven days, and you can point to a real, measurable change within thirty — or you get the month back. No moving goalposts, no debate.

1. The 7-day go-live promise

Your first AI team is in your stack and doing real work within seven days of the kickoff call. If we miss day seven for reasons within our control, that month is free — you are not billed for a month in which we failed to deliver the first build on time.

2. The 30-day measurable-savings refund

On the kickoff call we agree on one metric and a measurement window (30 days by default). The metric is something you can see — recovered hours, faster response or intake time, fewer dropped leads or reschedules, or dollarized savings. We commit to it in writing before we start, so there is no moving goalpost later.

If by day 30 that metric has not moved, your first month is refunded in full. You keep the dashboard access through the period you paid for. One agreed metric, one clear window, one honest answer.

3. Month-to-month, cancel anytime

  • All plans are month-to-month. There is no setup fee and no long-term contract.
  • You may cancel at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • Beyond the 30-day measurable-savings refund above, we do not refund partial months or unused portions of a billing period.
  • Custom or enterprise arrangements may carry separate terms defined in a written agreement.

4. How to request a refund

Email contact@efficio.tech from the address on your account within 5 days of the end of your measurement window. Reference the metric we agreed to on the kickoff call. We process approved refunds to your original Stripe payment method within 5–10 business days.

5. Where this fits

This policy is the financial guarantee referenced in our Terms of Service (Section 3). For how billing works across the AI Specialist, AI Team, and AI Department tiers, see pricing. Questions before you start? Book a 15-minute call.
