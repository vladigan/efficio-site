Show the math & assumptions no black box

Every number above comes straight from what you typed, multiplied by a few clearly-stated, deliberately conservative assumptions. Here’s exactly how it’s calculated:

1We convert weeks to months at 4.33 weeks per month (52 ÷ 12).
2Hours reclaimed assumes an AI team takes 60% of your routine admin, scheduling & follow-up off your plate — not all of it. The rest still needs a human.
So: your hours/week × 4.33 × 60%.
3Leads slipping through = your weekly leads × the % you entered. We assume we recover half (50%) of those — the rest were never reachable. Recovering even half is the conservative case.
4Revenue recovered = recovered leads/week × 4.33 × your average revenue per customer. We don’t assume every recovered lead becomes a sale beyond that 50% capture rate — no extra close-rate magic layered on top.
5Value of reclaimed time = reclaimed hours × the hourly value you entered. The monthly upside simply adds recovered revenue + that time value. Annual figure is × 12.

These percentages are our own conservative planning assumptions, not measured benchmarks or claims about typical results — your real numbers depend on your business, your tools, and how much you hand over. The point isn’t a precise promise; it’s a defensible, in-the-open ballpark you can sanity-check yourself.
