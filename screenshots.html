What's our refund policy for a customer on month 6 of annual billing?
For annual billing, prorated refunds are available within the first 60 days only. At month 6, the customer is outside the refund window. Options: credit toward next renewal, pause subscription, or downgrade. source: Finance Playbook · Refunds · v3.2
What if they're closing the business?
Business closure is covered under the "Hardship Exception" clause — up to 50% prorated refund available with director approval. File via the refund request form, tag @brady for sign-off. source: Finance Playbook · Exceptions · v3.2

Plain-English answers against your SOPs, past tickets, Notion docs. Every answer cites the source. When it doesn't know, it says so and suggests where to look.
