Why Us

We don't sell AI.
We ship it, in your business, within weeks.

There are a thousand AI consultancies now. Most hand you a strategy PDF and a login to someone else's tool. We run AI systems across four of our own businesses, and we build the same thing for yours — owned by you, priced for a small operator, and working inside your stack within the month.

The Difference

Built by operators who run the same tools they sell.

Efficio is run by Brady, an operator currently running four businesses on custom AI systems — a custom-AI agency (this one), a job-application SaaS with 1,000+ automated applications sent, a government-contract sourcing operation, and an automated trading system. Everything we build for clients is built on the same foundations we use on ourselves.

That matters because the difference between AI that works and AI that looks like it works isn't the model — it's the operator decisions around it. Where do you draw the line between automated and human-reviewed? What do you do when the agent is wrong? How do you know when you can trust a number on the dashboard? We've made those decisions for our own businesses dozens of times. That's the experience you're renting.

How We Engage

The working relationship, in plain English.

Discovery call. Free, 20 minutes. We ask what's costing you time, what you've already tried, and what your data looks like. If we can't help, we say so and point you somewhere that can.

Scope & proposal. If there's a fit, we come back with a written scope: what we're building, what it costs, what "done" looks like. Fixed price on the build, no change-order ambushes.

Kickoff & week 1. Access provisioned, first working cut by end of week one. You see it early, you tell us what's wrong, we fix it.

Ship & iterate. Most builds are done in 2–4 weeks. If you want us to keep extending it, we roll into a retainer. If you want us to hand it off and disappear, we document and go.

Retainer (optional). Monthly, month-to-month, cancel anytime. Useful when you've got a steady stream of "wouldn't it be nice if..." — we turn those into shipping features.
